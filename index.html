About

Craft, clarity, and care

I'm Yvonne So, a design leader with 18+ years of experience across UX, digital platforms, product, and service design. I specialize in tackling complex problems with deep customer empathy, translating ambiguity into clarity, and driving user value and business impact. I guide teams, lead large-scale programs, shape strategy, and stay hands-on as needed, working from vision to execution and down to the pixels.

I'm a people-first, purpose-driven leader who believes great work happens when people feel empowered, supported, and seen. I lead with authenticity, transparency, and trust, and I aim to build inclusive, collaborative environments where teams do their best work and design craft can thrive.

Colleagues often describe me as an exceptional systems thinker who brings structure to complexity and rallies teams around a shared vision. I balance strategy with execution, connecting design decisions to outcomes while keeping customers at the center.
