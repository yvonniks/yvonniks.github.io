Get a job done quicker with QuickTasks

QuickTasks is a social app that focuses on the immediacy of a job need done. We match service providers to clients based on needs, pricing requirements, location, and availability.

Try It Now

LOOKING FOR A SERVICE?

Post a request and get matched to professional service providers that can best help you. 

We simplify the process of looking for help. Instead of googling through a plethora of service providers, you can specify your location, date, and time requirements to narrow down your search.  Instead of calling multiple services to get a quote or trying to make an appointment, we provide the quoted price and next available time from an Intuit recommended service provider. 

CONVENIENT CONNECTION

Matched within 24 hours
We will find a service provider match for you (delivered via email or SMS) as soon as your request is submitted.

View ratings and reviews  
We pull information from Yelp so customers can assess the service they'd be getting based on what others in the community have said.   

Easy and speedy
Get the service you need immediately. No need to do extensive searching, and no need to make an appointment in advance.

HOW IT WORKS

Submit a request for a service you need as soon as possible
Enter your email or phone number
We’ll get back to you with a match to a recommended service provider* based on your request
* Intuit verified recommendations only available for plumbers at this time.
All other service providers may not be Intuit verified.
